I am a scientist in the Data Analytics Group at QCRI, Qatar Foundation. My current research mainly focuses on scalable cross-platform data management. Before joining QCRI, I was research associate in the Information Systems Group at Saarland University, where I worked with Jens Dittrich on big data management. During my Ph.D. at ATLAS-GDD, INRIA (University of Nantes), I worked (under the supervision of Philipe Lamarre and Patrick Valduriez) on distributed query processing in large-scale systems.
